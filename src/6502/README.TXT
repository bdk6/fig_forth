FIG6502.ASM is the original FIG listing, for a Rockwell native assembler.
FIG6502.ASX is my modified listing, which will assemble
  with MAS65 macro cross assembler, available from
  http://www2.whidbey.net/~beattidp/

FIG6502.as65 Is my translation of FIG6502.ASX for assembly with 
the AS6500 assembler available from

https://shop-pdp.net/index.php

and written (mostly) by Alan R. Baldwin at Kent State University.

The assembler is part of a package/family of assemblers and 
I have included the source in this repo for convenience and 
if the site above moves or disappears.
